Arduino Clinostat

For my science fair project of 2024, I created a DIY clinostat using CAD, 3D printing, and Arduino to simulate gravitropism in microgravity and test the effects of constant rotation on plant germination. I first designed a clinostat stand in OnShape, a free CAD program, and then learned to use Arduino and motors to keep it constantly rotating during the trial period 1 week over 3 trials. This project made it to MSEF and earned me the STS Sponsored MSEF Association of Women Geoscientists’s Student Award of Geoscience Excellence.
